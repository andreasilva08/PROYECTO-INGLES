Who we are

Estate of soul
peasant in
Santander

In the Guaure district of Mogotes, Santander, Cabañas El Cedro was born: a place built with materials from the region using techniques inherited from generations of farmers who refuse to disappear.

Here, the mountains, the forest, and the crystal-clear Guaure River coexist—already known to visitors seeking what they rarely find: nature without intermediaries and genuine hospitality.
